about

A student by degree, a designer by practice.

I'm a final-year Computer Science Engineering student who spends as much time in Figma as in a code editor. Over the past while I've worked with small businesses and brands — clinics, e-commerce stores, and product teams — building websites that look considered and actually work.

I like projects where design and engineering meet: a layout that's thought through, and code that ships it properly. Whether it's a full e-commerce build or a focused landing page, I treat every project like it's going in front of real customers — because it usually is.
